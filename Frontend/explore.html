What is StudyFort?

StudyFort is a modern, web-based academic management platform that simplifies how students and faculty collaborate. It brings together all essential academic tools — from notes to online classes — in one easy-to-use interface.

Unified Platform

No more juggling between WhatsApp, Gmail, or Google Meet — StudyFort keeps everything organized in one place.

Smart Uploads

Upload notes, assignments, and resources with a single click. Faculty can manage submissions effortlessly.

Seamless Communication

Enhance collaboration with integrated chat, group workspaces, and notifications for upcoming tasks.

Analytics Dashboard

Faculty can track attendance, submission rates, and performance trends using visual analytics tools.

Online Classes

Join or schedule live sessions directly via Google Meet or Zoom integration.

Multilingual Support

StudyFort can support multiple languages for better accessibility, especially for rural learners.
